Introduction

Welcome to Glus Family, your trusted E-commerce platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

When you register or make a purchase, we collect information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details. We may collect additional information you provide during customer support interactions.

Usage Information:

We collect data on how you interact with our app, including pages viewed, features used, and time spent.

Device Information:

We may collect information about your device, including the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

Location Information:

With your consent, we may collect precise location data for features such as order tracking.

How We Use Your Information

Order Processing:

We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders.

Personalization:

We personalize your experience based on your preferences and usage patterns.

Communications:

We may send you order updates, promotional offers, and newsletters. You can opt-out of marketing communications.

Analytics:

We use analytics to improve our app, services, and user experience.

Sharing Your Information

Service Providers:

We may share your information with trusted service providers for purposes such as payment processing and order fulfillment.

Legal Compliance:

We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or respond to law enforcement requests.

Your Choices

Account Information:

You can review and update your account information in the app settings.

Marketing Preferences:

You can manage your marketing communication preferences in the app.

Location Data:

You can control the app's access to location data through your device settings.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Glus Family is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or other means.
